What is the primary function of a cantilever in modern skyscraper design?

Your Selection

To increase the vertical load-bearing capacity of the central core.

Correct Solution

To provide structural support for overhanging sections without external columns.

Why this is correct

A cantilever is a rigid structural element that extends horizontally and is supported at only one end. In skyscrapers, this allows architects to create dramatic overhangs and open floor plans without the need for visual obstructions like exterior support columns.
